About Us

Welcome to IEkart - a student-centric e-commerce platform built exclusively for the IET Lucknow community. Our goal is to provide a safe, convenient, and reliable marketplace for students to buy and sell pre-owned items such as books, gadgets, bicycles, and more.

Our Mission

At IEkart, we aim to reduce waste, promote affordable student transactions, and create a trusted platform where students can trade items securely within the campus.

Why Choose IEkart?

  • Secure Transactions: Only verified IET Lucknow students can use the platform.
  • Cost Savings: Buy pre-owned items at a fraction of the original cost.
  • Eco-Friendly: Promote sustainability by reusing items.
  • Easy to Use: A simple, intuitive platform tailored for students.

How It Works?

  1. Sign Up: Create an account with your IET Lucknow email.
  2. List an Item: Upload details and set a price for your pre-owned item.
  3. Buy & Sell: Browse listings, chat with sellers, and complete transactions.
  4. Meet & Exchange: Arrange a meetup within campus to exchange the item.
