Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. What is IET Lucknow Marketplace?

→ IET Lucknow Marketplace is a secure online platform where students can buy and sell pre-owned items within the college community.

2. Who can use this platform?

→ Only students, faculty, and staff of IET Lucknow can register and use this marketplace.

3. How do I list an item for sale?

→ After logging in, go to the 'Sell an Item' section, provide details, upload images, and submit your listing.

4. Is it free to use?

→ Yes, listing and buying items on the platform are completely free for the IET Lucknow community.

5. How do I contact the seller?

→ Once you find an item you're interested in, you can contact the seller through the platform's messaging system.

6. What types of items can be sold?

→ Students can sell books, electronics, bicycles, furniture, and other college essentials. Illegal or restricted items are not allowed.

7. How can I report an issue?

→ If you encounter any problems or suspicious activity, please report it via the 'Contact Us' page.
